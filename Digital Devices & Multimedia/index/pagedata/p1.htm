Digital Devices and Multimedia

The key learning objectives for this simulation are:

  • List several ways to edit and print photos
  • Compare different methods for transferring images from a digital camera
  • Decide the best resolution for print or web media

You have volunteered to work with a nonprofit community beautification committee in your town. You have been assigned to a project that will focus on cleaning up the empty lot that residents and visitors see as they enter your community.

Your supervisor has assigned you the task of documenting the project over the next few weeks. Here are your responsibilities:

  • Collect low-resolution photos and videos to distribute on a social networking site.
  • Collect high-resolution photos for a feature article on the project that will be in the local newspaper.
